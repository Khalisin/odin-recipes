Lasagna

Description

This recipe is described as the world's best lasagna and is among the most popular recipes on allrecipes, garnering over 7 million views each year. The recipe is simple, consisting of common ingredients. The instructions are detailed and easy to follow, and it also provides instructions for how to freeze the lasagna and delicious recipes that pair well with the lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the Ricotta Mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover with Foil and Bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before covering

Lasagna Layering Instructions

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining parmesan
