Broccoli Cheese Soup

Description

This flavorful broccoli cheese soup recipe is great for luncheons or special gatherings. It is a simple recipe with common ingredients that does not require any special tools or knowledge, perfect for any family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 16 oz chopped frozen broccoli
  • 4 cans chicken broth
  • 1 loaf processed cheese food
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 2/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened. Stir in broccoli. Add broth and simmer until broccoli is tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Reduce heat; add cheese cubes and stir until melted. Stir in milk and garlic powder.
  3. Stir cornstarch and water together in a small bowl until smooth. Stir into soup; cook, stirring frequently, until thick.
