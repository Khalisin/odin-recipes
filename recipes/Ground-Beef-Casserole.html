Ground Beef Casserole

Description

This ground beef casserole recipe is homemade and comforting, and is simple to make. The recipe is intended to use any leftovers in the fridge to make a delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Seasonings
  • Pasta
  • Sour Cream and Cream Cheese
  • Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in a skillet over medium-heat until it's brown and crumbly. Drain and discard the grease. Mix tomato sauce, sugar, garlic salt, and salt into the ground beef and simmer until the flavors blend. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
  2. Boil the egg noodles until they're cooked through, but are still quite firm. Drain in a colander and allow to cool slightly.
  3. Mix the sour cream, cream cheese, and diced onion in a medium bowl until well-combined.
  4. Layer half of the egg noodles into a greased casserole dish. Top with half of the sour cream mixture, then half of the beef mixture. Repeat in the same order. Sprinkle the top layer of beef with cheese. Bake until the cheese is melted and golden, about 25-30 minutes.
